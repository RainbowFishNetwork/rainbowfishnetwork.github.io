2022.2.25英语笔记

发表于2022-02-25|更新于2022-02-25
|字数总计:294|阅读时长:1分钟|阅读量:

这是一个测试文档，格式待定

stress
adj. stressed / stressful
I am stressed out
a stressful job

waste
the waste of time
waste time on sth. / sb.
waste sometime (in) doing sth.
e.g.
Please don’t waste time in playing computer games
Please don’t waste time on computer games

in order to
in order to do sth. = so as to do = to do
in order not to do sth. = so as not to do = not to do
in order that + 从句 = so that + 从句
e.g.
In order to catch the school bus, I get up early every morning.
In order not to miss the school bus, I get up early every morning
We study hard in order that we can get into a good senior high school
We study hard so that we can get into a good senior high school
we study hard to get into a good senior high school
We study hard in order to get into a good senior high school
We study hard so as to get into a good senior high school

depend
adj. dependent
n. dependence
adj. independent
n. independence
depend on
e.g. Young people shouldn’t depend on their parents.
That depends. / It (all) depends
e.g.
——Will he come?
——That depends. / It depends. He may not have time.

fair
n. fairness
adj. unfair
n. unfairness
e.g.
It also helps them to understand the idea of fairness
Was it fair for him to ask him to do all the work?,
He always get more than me. It’s unfair
There will be a book fair next week
